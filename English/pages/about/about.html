📌 About Me – Echo World

🚀 My Passion is Building Professional Websites with the Best Technologies!

Hello! I am an independent web developer specializing in HTML, CSS, PHP, and MySQL. I design and develop professional, responsive, and fast websites that meet my clients' needs and help them achieve digital success.

📌 Work Rules & Client Requirements

Welcome! To ensure your project is executed professionally and smoothly, please review the following guidelines before we begin.

📌 Project Details

  • A clear description of the website is required, including the number of pages, design preferences, and features (e.g., booking, contact forms, etc.).
  • Specify whether the website is Static or Dynamic (with a database).
  • Hosting and domain purchase are not included in the service; the client must provide them.

    • 💰 Pricing & Payment

  • The cost is determined based on project requirements.
  • A 50% upfront payment is required before starting, with the remaining balance due upon final delivery.
  • Any additional features requested after the agreement will be priced separately.

    • 🚫 Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • If the project is canceled after work has started, the upfront payment is non-refundable, as it covers time and effort.
  • If canceled before work begins, a partial refund may be issued based on the agreement.

    • 🔄 Revisions & Modifications

  • A limited number of free revisions is included; additional revisions will incur extra charges.
  • Revisions cover design improvements or bug fixes, but do not include major project changes beyond the original agreement.

    • 📦 Delivery & Ownership Rights

  • After full payment, the client receives all rights to the final website files.
  • My developer credit must not be removed unless agreed upon in advance.

    • 🛠️ Post-Delivery Support

  • 7 days of free technical support is provided to resolve any issues.
  • After the free support period, ongoing maintenance services can be arranged for an additional fee.

    • ⏳ Timeline

    The completion time depends on project size, and any additional modifications may extend the timeline.


    📌 Client Requirements

    📝 Website Content

  • Text content (service descriptions, company info, terms of use, etc.).
  • Required images and logos.
  • Contact details to be displayed.

    • 🎨 Design Preferences

  • Any reference websites or inspirations for design.
  • Preferred colors and branding guidelines.

    • 🌐 Hosting & Domain

  • The client is responsible for purchasing hosting and domain.
  • I can provide consultation on the best options, but purchasing and setup are not part of my service.

    • 📊 Additional Requirements

  • Do you need SEO optimization for better search engine visibility?
  • Do you require integration with social media or third-party platforms?

    • 🌍 Required Languages

    If the website is multilingual, the translated content for each language must be provided.
